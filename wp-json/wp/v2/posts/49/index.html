What is Scite?<\/strong><\/h4>\n

Scite is an AI-powered platform that helps researchers and students discover and evaluate scientific articles via Smart Citations. Smart Citations allow users to see how a publication has been cited by providing the context of the citation and a classification describing whether it provides supporting or contrasting evidence for the cited claim1.<\/p>\n

The platform has extracted and analyzed 1.2 billion citation statements from 187 million articles, book chapters, preprints, and datasets. It is trusted by leading universities, publishers, and corporations across the world.<\/p>\n

This tool is useful for students and academic researchers in several ways. For instance, it can help researchers track and improve research outcomes across their organization. It can also help researchers see exactly how publications have been cited by newer research1. This can be useful for evaluating the quality of articles and ensuring that the research is up-to-date.<\/p>\n

Moreover, it can help universities and governments track and improve research outcomes across their organization. This can be useful for evaluating the impact of research and identifying areas for improvement.<\/p>\n

This tool can also help publishers improve the discoverability of their publications and support their authors. By providing Smart Citations, it can help readers understand the context of a citation and whether it provides supporting or contrasting evidence for the cited claim.<\/p>\n
